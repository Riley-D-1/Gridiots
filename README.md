# Gridiots
A online battle simulator filled with the most serious of characters, think tabs style game. Sick of being told you can only place on one side?
These gridiots fight each other using the power of stupidity, place your people in strategic ways to get bonuses and protect agains the enemy's squares. Control the most gridiots by the end of the 5th round to win!. 


Rules
<h3>Rules for Gridiots</h3> 
    <ol>
        <li> The game shows both players</li>
        <li> Each round you are shown the amount of gridiots you can place and then can then place them </li>
        <li> Your enemy will then place theirs!</li>
        <li> A battle will ensue with your gridiots doing better if next to friends (vertically and horizontally not diagonally)</li>
        <li> The rounds will repeat for 5 rounds</li>
        <li>Whoever has the most area wins!</li>
    <ol>

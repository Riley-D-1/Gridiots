Rules for Gridiots

  1. The game shows both players
  2. Each round you are shown the amount of gridiots you can place and then can then place them
  3. Your enemy will then place theirs!
  4. A battle will ensue with your gridiots doing better if next to friends (vertically and horizontally not diagonally)
  5. The rounds will repeat for 5 rounds
  6. Whoever has the most area wins!
